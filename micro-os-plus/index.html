µOS++ IIIe

Last modified on Sun Jun 26 20:36:06 2016 UTC.

Overview

The µOS++ IIIe (micro oh ɛs plus plus third edition) project (affectionately called Ilie, Romanian for Eliyahu/Илия́/Elijah, also the protector of aviators) is the third iteration of µOS++, an open source, royalty-free, multi-tasking operating system intended for 8/16/32/64 bit embedded systems, written in C++.

The corner stone of µOS++ IIIe is CMSIS++ RTOS API, and the relationship is reciprocal, since µOS++ provides CMSIS++ the reference implementation for the CMSIS++ syncronisation objects (mutex, semaphore, etc) and for the CMSIS++ scheduler.
